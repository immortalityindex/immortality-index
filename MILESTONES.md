## Track 01 · Genetics

### Phase 1 — Reprogramming Layer

#### G-1.1 · In Vivo Epigenetic Reprogramming Proof-of-Concept

**Theoretical Target:** Demonstrate safe, reversible, partial epigenetic reprogramming in a living human subject that measurably reverses cellular age markers (Horvath DNA methylation clock) by ≥20 years without inducing pluripotency-associated oncogenesis or teratoma formation.

**Milestones:**
1. Non-oncogenic partial reprogramming protocol (OSK without c-Myc) validated in human organoids — independent lab replication confirmed
2. In vivo murine model shows ≥20% reversal on Horvath DNA methylation clock with zero tumor incidence over 12-month follow-up
3. Phase I human safety IND filing accepted — cyclic OSKM/OSK expression trialled in an isolated somatic tissue type (e.g., retinal ganglion cells)
4. Peer-reviewed evidence of functional tissue rejuvenation (>15% improvement in tissue-specific functional assay) in non-human primate without teratoma at 18 months

**Milestones:**

## Track 02 · Nanotech

### Phase 1 — Propulsion & Processing Layer

#### N-1.1 · Remote Mechanical Micro-Propulsion

**Theoretical Target:** Demonstrate a sub-5-micron self-propelled unit capable of navigating the human cardiovascular system against physiological flow at ≥0.5 cm/s under remote external guidance (magnetic or acoustic), reaching a designated target site within ±50 µm accuracy from an external control source at ≥10 cm distance — without vascular occlusion or haemolysis.

**Milestones:**
1. Helical magneto-acoustic micro-swimmer (≤3 µm) navigates in microfluidic channels simulating capillary flow at ≥0.5 cm/s against-flow velocity — independently replicated in ≥2 labs
2. 3D in vitro vascular phantom navigation: unit reaches designated target site within ±50 µm accuracy under rotating magnetic field guidance from 10 cm external source with real-time imaging feedback
3. In vivo biodistribution study in murine model: >60% of administered units reach target organ within 4 hours of IV administration without vascular occlusion events
4. Acoustic + magnetic hybrid steering demonstrated for CNS penetration — units cross validated in vitro blood-brain barrier model (TEER >200 Ω·cm²) without compromising barrier integrity

**Milestones:**

2. Molecular memristor array (MoS₂ or organic polymer-based) achieves ≤10 aJ per state transition with >10⁶ switching endurance cycles — retention >10 years at 37°C projected
3. Neuromorphic spike-based processing unit at ≤10 aJ/spike performs binary biomarker classification (target vs. non-target cell surface receptor pattern) with >95% accuracy in bench test
4. Integrated nano-computing module (10 aJ processing + local molecular memory) validated in full biological simulation pipeline: detect → decide → signal → execute therapeutic action

## Notes on Framework

Several concepts in this index — including **Boss Hurdles**, **P2P Re-Flashing**, **Cryptographic Logic Gate Wiring**, and the **12-hour operational window** — are original theoretical constructs developed for this roadmap. They are not established scientific terminology. They represent engineering targets derived from extrapolating current biological and nanotechnology research trajectories.

This index operates from a transhumanist perspective — treating biological aging as an engineering problem, not an inevitability. Milestone completion reflects an AI system's interpretation of available published research, not a formal peer review or expert panel evaluation.
